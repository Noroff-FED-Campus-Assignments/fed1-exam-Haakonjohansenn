About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Haakon, and I'm a 26-year-old adventurer who loves exploring the unknown parts of the world. I'm excited to share with you my journey of starting a blog about my hikes and travels, and how it has changed my life in unexpected ways. It all started a few years ago, when I took a solo trip to South America. I had always been interested in hiking and nature, but this trip was a turning point for me. I found myself drawn to the rugged beauty of the Andes and the Amazon rainforest, and I spent most of my days trekking through remote trails, camping in the wild, and meeting fascinating people from different cultures.

Slowly but surely, my blog started to grow. I shared my posts on social media, and I received positive feedback from friends and strangers alike. People were inspired by my adventures, and they wanted to know more.. I felt a sense of purpose and connection that I had never felt before.
